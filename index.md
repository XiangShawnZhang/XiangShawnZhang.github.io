## About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Geography, McGill University. My research fields include Environmental Economics, Well-being, and Policy Evaluation. Specifically, I am interested in people's well-being and equity impacts from environmental and energy policy. Currently, I am working on a program focus on welfare impacts of the "Clean Heating Program" in rural North China. My supervisors are [Brian E Robinson](https://brianerobinson.com/) and [Christopher Barrington-Leigh](https://wellbeing.research.mcgill.ca/index.php?m=about).

## Research Interests

- **Environmental Economics:** air pollution, energy transition, environmental equity
- **Policy Evaluation :** casual inference, quasi-experiment design
